best kerala food

puttu and Kadala Curry

As cute as its name sounds, puttu is a cylindrical steamed rice cake cooked with coconut shavings

appam with stew

Ask anybody from Kerala what their favourite dish is from back home and they will swear by appam and stew!

idli sambar

Idli Sambar is a hearty, satisfying, comforting and a healthy meal of soft fluffy idlis served with savory, spiced and lightly tangy sambar

kerala prawn curry

If you are in Kerala and you miss out on the phenomenal prawn curry, then your trip will remain incomplete.

malabar porotta

This is a mouth-watering street food originating from the Malabar region of the coast.

ethakka appam(banana fritters)

Pazham Pori or Ethakka Appam is juicy banana fritters that are a favourite tea time snack in Kerala.

idiyappam with curry

Idiyappam is basically made of rice flour, water and salt. Numerous strands of vermicelli are entwined together to make this version of an appam.

erissery(pumpkin lentil stew)

Filled with healthy ingredients of gram and pumpkin, is one of the traditional kootan in Kerala cuisine.
